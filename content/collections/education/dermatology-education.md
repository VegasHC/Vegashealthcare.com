They are also experts in managing hair and nail disorders, as these are closely related to the health of the skin.'

            text: 'The skin is a fascinating organ that serves as a barrier between your body and the external environment. It has multiple layers, each with its own unique characteristics and functions. The outermost layer, called the epidermis, acts as a protective shield against harmful substances and UV radiation. The dermis, the middle layer, contains blood vessels, sweat glands, hair follicles, and nerve endings. The subcutaneous tissue, the deepest layer, provides insulation and cushioning for your organs.'
